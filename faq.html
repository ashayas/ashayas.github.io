Frequently asked questions

What is Honeycomb?

Honeycomb is a food matchmaking service that matches people with specific restaurant menu items based on their personal preferences and dietary restrictions.

Who is it for?

Honeycomb is for people like us. That is, people who have food restrictions and suffer the anxiety of not knowing where to eat or what to eat there. There's just too much risk involved in making a mistake, so we made Honeycomb to make our lives easier. Hopefully we make your life easier too!

How does it work?

We'll ask you a few questions about your dietary preferences and you'll be connected to our system. Once you choose a restaurant to be matched with, you will receive a personalized menu with the most suitable items to eat at that restaurant. 

Do I have to pay for it?

Nope, it's free! Isn't that great?

What type of restaurants can I match with?

We currently have over 200 over Vancouver's favourite restaurants listed in our app. Stay tuned for our announcements of new restaurants we're currently adding into our system.

Can I suggest a restaurant?

Absolutely! We'd love to accommodate the restaurants you're interested in. To suggest a restaurant, please email us at chef@honeycomb.ai

Do you have a mobile app?

Our app will be available early February! Request an invitation and we'll notify you sooner than anyone else ;)
