Frequently asked questions

What is Honeycomb?

Honeycomb is a food matchmaking service that matches people with specific restaurant menu items based on their personal preferences and dietary restrictions.

Who is it for?

Honeycomb is for people like us. That is, people who have food restrictions and suffer the anxiety of not knowing where to eat or what to eat there. There's just too much risk involved in making a mistake, so we made Honeycomb to make our lives easier. Hopefully we make your life easier too!

How does it work?

Create a profile in 60 seconds. We'll ask you a few questions and you'll be connected to our system. Once you choose a restaurant to be matched with, you will receive a personalized menu with the most suitable items to eat at that restaurant. 

Please note that we are currently adding more restaurants into our system, we'll have some more great places up shortly. Stay tuned for awesome announcements and follow us on social media.

Do I have to pay for it?

Not now, not ever. Honeycomb will always be 100% free for customers.

What type of restaurants can I match with?

We only partner with quality restaurants that emphasize their interest in catering to customers with food restrictions. Stay tuned for our announcements of new restaurants we're currently adding into our system.

Can I suggest a restaurant?

Absolutely! We'd love to accommodate the restaurants you're interested in. To suggest a restaurant, please click here.

Do you have a mobile app?

Our app will be available shortly. Please follow us on social media so you can be notified when we launch our app!
