about honeycomb

we’re food matchmakers

we make eating out easy for people with food restrictions

Honeycomb is an online food matchmaking service based in Vancouver, Canada. We have designed a method of helping consumers afflicted with dietary needs identify suitable menu items at local restaurants.

For consumers with food restrictions, going out to eat is a serious matter. The wrong choice of dish can be the difference between a pleasant night out or a trip to the hospital. Our team has had to endure these experiences first hand, which is why we created Honeycomb as a necessity in the marketplace.

Honeycomb is not a mindless algorithm. We are simply people looking to help those who will benefit from the assurance of someone they can trust. That is why we handpick the restaurants on our platform, and make sure they live up to the quality and standards that we can put our full faith into. 

With a collective experience in restaurant marketing, operations, and service, our Honeycomb team is currently deploying our platform in North America.

our story

the tale of how it all began

It all started almost three years ago, when I was diagnosed with an auto-immune condition that affected my colon. At first, I shrugged it off like it was no big deal, and I really thought it wasn’t. In fact, most of my symptoms weren't even severe enough to faze me. “What’s that, Doc? I’m more susceptible to colon cancer? Eh, I’ll worry about that in twenty years…” Maybe I was ignorant or overconfident or too busy to take it seriously – perhaps I was all three. I know only one thing: I had absolutely no idea of what was to come, and how drastically my life would change over the next three years. This is my story of how I went from cocky teenager to hospital patient to entrepreneur with a dream of helping those who suffer like I have. This is the story of Honeycomb.
