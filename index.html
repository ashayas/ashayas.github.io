I don't feel the need to showcase technical projects here anymore so instead here are some of my poems:

Aurora Borealis

Vivid pastels grace the night.

Illumination.

Abstraction in all its purity,

senses temporarily hindered

So far yet so bright.

All I do is stare and ...

wait.

The surreality confounds me,

all who marvel at this

glorious sight are right

But.

I am not as amused.

You. You are my northern light

Sick

You know what makes me sick?

The bone aching, muscle fasiculations sick.

The apnea inducing, gasping for air in the middle of the night sick.

The being shackled and unable to help vulnerability sick.

The tumultuous thoughts racketeering in my head sick.

The post 400m sprint sick.

The being away from you sick.

The waiting lobby sick.
