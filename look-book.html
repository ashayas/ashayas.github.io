Look Book

Look Book is an app proposal for a mall, client of RC Comunicação, agency where the project was conceived. The main idea was to spread to the fashion public the possibilities of look creations by using clothes of the shops in the mall. The looks would be created by influencers, the mall shops and the users themselves, reaching the objective of connect these three publics and make the mall a fashion reference. Here my role was to create all UX and UI design around the main idea. Unfortunately the project stops in prototype.
